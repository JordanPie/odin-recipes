Lemon Butter Chicken

Description

Golden brown pan seared chicken breasts coated in a quick lemon pan sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth
  • Lemon
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Season both sides of the chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Dredge chicken breasts in flour
  3. melt butter in pan
  4. sear until golden brown
  5. reduce to med heat add garlic saute 20 seconds
  6. add chicken broth and lemon juice, bring to a simmer until reduced by half
  7. add in 3Tbsp butter and lemon zest, stir to melt buter
  8. return chicken to skillet, spoon sauce over chicken and garnish with parsley
